About the Game Character Tracker

Welcome to the Game Character Tracker! This platform allows players to easily track their stats in three popular games: Apex Legends & lol.

Our Mission

Created for the WDD330 course at BYU-I, this project gives access to in-game statistics, helping players improve their performance and keep track of their progress. The app pulls real-time data, allowing players to search by name and platform for accurate stats.

Games Covered

    Apex Legends: Track kills, wins, damage, and more!

    lol: View stats including overall performance and leaderboard standing.

Built by

This project was developed by Jeremy Oliver as part of the WDD330 course at BYU-I.
